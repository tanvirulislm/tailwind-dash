Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our platform

How do I enroll in a course?

Enrolling in a course is simple! Just browse our course catalog, select the course you're interested in, and click the "Enroll Now" button. You'll be guided through the payment process (if applicable) and gain immediate access to the course materials.

Do you offer financial aid or scholarships?

Yes! We offer need-based scholarships for select programs. To apply, visit our Scholarships page and complete the application form. We also offer flexible payment plans for many of our courses, allowing you to pay in installments.

Can I access courses on mobile devices?

Absolutely! Our platform is fully responsive and works on smartphones and tablets. You can download our mobile app (available for iOS and Android) for an optimized learning experience on the go.

How long do I have access to a course?

All our courses come with lifetime access. Once you enroll, you can revisit the material anytime, even after completing the course. We also update course content regularly, and you'll get access to all future updates at no additional cost.

Are the certificates recognized by employers?

Our certificates are widely recognized in the industry. Many of our students have used them to advance their careers or land jobs at top companies. Some of our career-track programs even come with direct hiring partnerships.
